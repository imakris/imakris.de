Scheduled calls

A scheduled call can be arranged by email.

To request a call, include three proposed time slots, with time zone, within a specified date range - for example, "morning CET, any time between the 20th and 27th of the month." I will confirm one of the three, counter-propose if none is workable, and send a calendar invitation for the agreed time.
